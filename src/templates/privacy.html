Privacy Policy

Please read this page in conjunction with our terms and conditions.

Email

  • A valid email address is required to sign up for an account.
  • We use your email address to send you important information about Anki and your account, such as a reset link when you forget your password.
  • We will never use your email for marketing emails or sell your data.

User Data

  • When you add cards via AnkiCollab, or use the synchronization feature in the Anki clients to send your data to AnkiCollab, we store the card data and the IP Address used to upload the data.
  • This personal data is not visible to other users of the service.
  • This data is visible to the dev team when investigating problems with the service, and problems you report.

Cookies

  • AnkiCollab uses an internet technology called "cookies" to keep you logged in to the website.
  • The cookies allow us to associate the actions you take with the correct account.
  • AnkiCollab may use Google Analytics for usage statistics, and it may have stored some tracking cookies on your computer.
  • AnkiCollab ignores any tracking cookies set by Google Analytics, and you can safely use your browser's cookie manager to delete them if you wish.

Logs and Error Reporting

  • All requests made to AnkiCollab, whether they be through a web browser or through one of the Anki clients, are logged.
  • We record the date and time, the page on AnkiCollab that is being accessed and your IP address.
  • Email-related operations may log your email address.
  • We store this information for security purposes (such as blocking malicious access attempts), for diagnosing problems, for analyzing usage of the service, and for providing support.
  • Logs and error reports are automatically sent and stored via secure means to our infrastructure.

Syncing

  • When you use the synchronization feature to transfer data between an Anki client and AnkiCollab, the client sends the hashed names of the subscribed decks as well as the timestamp of your last synchronization.
  • AnkiCollab does not store this information for longer than it needs to process the synchronization request.

User Support

  • For support requests, please join our discord server.

Locations

  • We use servers and cloud services located inside secure data centers in the European Union (Germany) to store your data.

Third-Party Sharing

  • The cards you add to AnkiCollab are public by default.
  • We may share your data with law enforcement authorities when required to do so by law.
  • As decks are contributed by other Anki users, they may contain links to third-party servers not under our control. This includes any images embedded in the deck or add-on descriptions - when you view a deck on AnkiCollab, your browser will fetch any images that are hosted on remote sites, and those remote sites will learn of your IP address and may send a tracking cookie when an image is fetched from them.

Data Retention

  • We will retain your user data while you continue to use the service.
  • If you wish to remove your user data, you can delete your AnkiCollab account. Just email us at ankicollab@proton.me from the email you used to register the account with and we will remove the account as soon as we can
  • If you delete your account, we will delete your user data.
  • Some of your user data may remain in backups and logs when your account is deleted.
  • We will retain information when legally required to do so.

Keeping Data Safe

  • We endeavor to keep your data safe by following best practices such as encrypting your data in transit, keeping machines up to date, and maintaining backups.
  • No system can ever be completely secure, so we strongly recommend you store a local copy of your user data. You can use the Export functionality in Anki clients in order to save a backup of your data.
  • As your password secures your account, please ensure you use a secure password and don't use the same password on multiple sites. If you don't already use one, we highly recommend a password manager like Bitwarden - they make life easier and keep you more secure.

Lawful Basis

Data processing laws require us to state the lawful basis we use for processing your data.

  • Data you provide to us such as your email address and card content is necessary for providing the service to you (servicing a contract).
  • Statistics that we collect from your data are used to improve the service, and for research (servicing a contract, and legitimate interests).
  • Access logs are used for diagnostic and analytical purposes, and for detecting misuse of the service (servicing a contract, and legitimate interests).

Changes

If significant changes to this policy are made, we will notify you via a message when visiting the website, a message when you use the synchronization feature, or via email.

Transfer

In the event of a company acquisition, restructure, or bankruptcy, we may transfer your data to a different legal entity. The new legal entity shall respect your privacy in the same way, and notify you if they wish to use your data in a way not described here.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please email us at ankicollab@proton.me
